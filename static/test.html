Context

Comments can be selected by deck ID and slide ID. During normal operation both identifiers will be determined from the deck source and should be calculated such that the tuple (deck ID, slide ID) uniquely identifies a slide (For a suitable definition of uniquely).

All matching questions are displayed, regardless of author. Questions that match the current author are rendered with a delete button.

Inputs are not yet validated aggressively. Tread lightly.
